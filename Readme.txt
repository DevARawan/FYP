how to start?
run 'yarn android' command on vs code terminal to create a build. Open emulator on android studio software. 
Once build in created and the metro server starts, press 'a'.It will open the app in emulator.
How to use ?
signup and login with your email and password.Add your goals information,add your income and expenses.
The system will compute savings and show your progress towards goals through circular progressbar.
you can update and delete goals. Once a goal in achieved the system congratulates you and celebrates your achievement.
you can view and update your personal profile.
you can visualize charts in settings screen in 'Financial summaries' option for better decision making.
you can view your achievements in 'settings' screen and share your achievements on social media.
you can choose to get loan by navigating to 'settings' screen selecting loans option.
you can choose to give a feedback in 'settings' screen using feedback option.
